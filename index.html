About IEEE RAS

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society focuses on the advancement of robotics, automation engineering, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, and intelligent machines. Our student branch chapter helps students learn, build, and collaborate through technical events, workshops, projects, and competitions.
